Chicken Tikka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  5. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers.
  6. Place the skewers on a baking tray and brush with oil.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  8. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
