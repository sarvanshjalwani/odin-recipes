Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Add the tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. In a separate pot, boil the rice until it is 70% cooked. Drain the water.
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken masala. Sprinkle fresh coriander and mint leaves on top.
  8. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  9. Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Gently fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot with raita or your favorite side dish.
